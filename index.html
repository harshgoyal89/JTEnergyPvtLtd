There is a Minor Differnce between them. In Residential solar plants are typically smaller (around 3kW to 10kW) and designed to meet the energy needs of a single home where Commercial solar plants are larger (10kW to several megawatts) to power businesses, factories, or large facilities.

How Solar Work?

A solar plant works by converting sunlight into electricity. Solar panels, made up of photovoltaic (PV) cells, capture sunlight and turn it into direct current (DC) electricity. An inverter then converts the DC electricity into alternating current (AC), which can be used to power homes, businesses, or be fed into the grid. Any excess energy can be stored in batteries or sent back to the grid for future use.

PM SURYA GHAR YOJANA

PM - Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

The PM Surya Ghar Yojana is an initiative by the Indian government aimed at promoting solar energy usage across the country. Here are some key points about the scheme:

1. **Objective**: The primary goal is to encourage the installation of solar rooftop systems in residential and commercial buildings, reducing reliance on conventional energy sources.

2. **Financial Support**: The scheme provides financial assistance and subsidies to make solar installations more affordable for citizens.

3. **Environmentally Friendly**: By promoting solar energy, the initiative helps reduce carbon footprints and combat climate change.

4.**Energy Independence**: It aims to increase energy self-sufficiency and reduce dependence on fossil fuels.

5. **Employment Generation**: The program is expected to create job opportunities in the renewable energy sector, contributing to economic growth.

6. **Awareness Campaigns**: The government is also working on educating the public about the benefits of solar energy and how to access the scheme.
